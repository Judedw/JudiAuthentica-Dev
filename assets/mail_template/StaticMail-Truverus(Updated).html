PEO's 2019 Council Elections

As an eligible voter in PEO's 2019 Council Elections, please find below
information on how you can participate in this very important process.

As a reminder, voting is available until 4:00 p.m. (EST) on February 22, 2019.

Candidate information, including platforms, webcasts and eblast material, is available at www.peovote.ca.

  • To access your ballot, visit www.peovote.ca and click on the VOTE NOW button.
  • Enter the ID provided below.
    ID: username
  • Enter your PIN below (a PIN can be used only once, to submit one ballot).
    PIN: password
  • Once you have accessed your ballot, carefully review the voting instructions, enter your selection and press the continue button.
